FAQ

Common questions

Is PDFtoDocHub really 100% free? +
Yes, completely free. No hidden charges, no subscription, no credit card needed. We earn through non-intrusive ads to keep the service running.
Do I need to create an account? +
No account, no email, no password. Just upload your file and convert. That's it.
Is my file kept private? +
Absolutely. Your files are automatically deleted from our servers within 5 minutes of conversion. We never read your documents or share them with anyone.
What file formats are supported? +
We support PDF files for PDF → Word conversion, and DOCX / DOC files for Word → PDF conversion. Maximum file size is 50 MB.
Does PDF → Word work on scanned documents? +
Our current converter works best on text-based (digitally created) PDFs. Scanned PDFs that are essentially images may not extract text correctly. OCR support is coming soon.
How many files can I convert per day? +
There's no daily limit. Convert as many files as you need, for free, all day long.
